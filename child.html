Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

BBBP scheme under Sambal sub-scheme of Mission Shakti aims to prevent Gender biased sex selective elimination, to ensure survival and protection of girl child and also to ensure education of the girl child

Balika Samridhi

This scheme covers girl children in families below the poverty line (BPL) in both rural and urban areas in all districts and states of India

Sukanya Samriddhi

SSY is a small deposit scheme by the Ministry of Finance exclusively for a girl child. The scheme is meant to meet the education and marriage expenses of a girl child

Ladli Scheme

The scheme focuses on creating a positive attitude towards the birth, health and education of the girl child. Also aims at providing monetary assistance to girl children in order to empower girls

Child Protection services

Child Protection Services (CPS) Scheme under which support is provided to States and UT Governments for delivering services for children in need and difficult circumstances

POSHAN Abhiyaan

It aims to address malnutrition issues across the country through components like ICT Application, Convergence, Behavioural Change & Jan Andolan, Capacity Building, Incentives and Awards
